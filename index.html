About

For over twenty years, we’ve honoured the essence of tradition, infusing each dish with our passion and authentic flavours since 1997.What began as a humble endeavour has evolved into a culinary institution, renowned for our commitment to preserving the essence of Hyderabadi cuisine. At Pista House, we don’t just serve food, we curate experiences that linger in the memory long after the last bite.Our secret? It lies in the meticulous craftsmanship that goes into every dish, from our world-famous Haleem to the iconic Biryani that melts in your mouth. Each recipe is a testament to generations of culinary expertise, passed down through our family with care and reverence.
